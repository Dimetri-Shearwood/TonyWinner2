ABOUT

Born in Chattanooga TN, and raised in St. Croix, in the Virgin Islands, Tony Winner is a producer, songwriter, artist, and musician. Even at a young age, he was always drawn to music. This love for music has led him to what can only be viewed as a successful career, such as playing the bass guitar for a Dove Award winning album, landing placements with multiple shows and commercials, including the 2021 Superbowl, and writing many great songs in a vast number of genres, such as pop, hip hop, and funk, just to name a few.

TV/FILM

Tony Winner's music has been featured in a number of TV shows, film trailers, and network promos.
